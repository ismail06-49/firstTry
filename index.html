About Me

Logistics Manager

A Logistics Manager is responsible for planning and organizing the flow of goods and services within the supply and distribution chain. They oversee logistics operations to ensure the efficient and effective allocation of resources, maximizing efficiency in delivering products from production to customers. Their role includes analyzing logistics data, managing inventory, coordinating transportation, and ensuring compliance with logistics standards.
